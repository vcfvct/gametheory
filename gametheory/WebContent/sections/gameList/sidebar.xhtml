  • Rules

    In this game two companies obtained permission to manufacture in a developing country on the premise of at least one must provide schooling or pay a fine if neither provides schooling.
    As players neither knows the action or moves of the other company other than profits will be diminished if school is provided and the profits will diminished less but still reduced with the fine plus the loss of reputation from a fine.
    Providing schooling for any two periods in a four period cycle will absolve a company of fines but two periods of fines in four years will lead to reparations of all investments."
